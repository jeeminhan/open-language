What It Does

Have a real conversation. Get real feedback.

Talk to an AI tutor that corrects you like a good friend would — naturally, not robotically. It tracks every error, learns your weak points, and steers future conversations toward what you need to practice.

open-language — Session #14
I goed to the store yesterday and buyed some fruits   Oh nice! I went to the farmer's market last weekend too.   What kind of fruit did you buy? I've been really into   persimmons lately. I buyed apples and some persimmon too!   Persimmons are so good this time of year! Just a quick   note — the past tense of "buy" is "bought", not "buyed".   It's irregular, like go/went. (Korean doesn't have   irregular verbs, so this is a tricky one!)   Do you prefer the crunchy or soft kind?

First time → models correctly in response. Second time → recasts prominently. Third time → explains gently.

Journal Mode

Write freely first. Learn from mistakes after.

Journal mode flips the script: you speak or type your thoughts with zero interruptions. The tutor stays silent — just a quiet "hmm" or "yeah" so you know it's listening. When you're done, you get a full error report with corrections.

Post-Journal Corrections

These appear only after you press "Done" — never during writing.

→
바쁜 하루였어요  바쁜 하루를 보냈어요
More natural phrasing — "spent a busy day" vs "it was a busy day"
→
됐으면  되었으면
Written Korean prefers the full form over the contracted spoken form
What you did well
+ Natural use of —(으)ㄴ 것 같아요 for expressing uncertainty
+ Correct honorific level throughout (polite —요 form)
+ Good topic variety and sentence connectors
Why journal mode?
1. Builds fluency — uninterrupted output trains your brain to produce language without pausing
2. Reduces anxiety — no fear of being corrected mid-thought
3. Better error quality — reviewing corrections after writing is more effective than real-time interruption
4. Captures natural speech — errors in free expression reveal your true level
Behind Every Turn

The tutor analyzes everything you say.

Every message you send is parsed for errors, grammar patterns, vocabulary, and fluency signals. This data feeds 9 learning loops that make the tutor smarter over time.

analysis (hidden from learner)
{ "errors": [{ "type": "irregular_past_tense", "observed": "buyed", "expected": "bought", "l1_source": "Korean has regular conjugation — no irregular forms" }], "grammar_used_correctly": [{ "pattern": "past_tense_marker", "level": "A2" }], "correction_action": "recast", "vocabulary_used": ["store","fruits"] }

What gets tracked:

Errors Categorized, counted, linked to L1 interference patterns
Grammar Correct and incorrect uses tracked for mastery percentage
Vocabulary Every unique word logged, unknown words flagged for review
Fluency Hesitation, self-correction, code-switching, confidence
Interests Books, music, hobbies detected — used to personalize topics
Self-Learning System

9 feedback loops that compound.

The tutor doesn't just respond — it evaluates itself, clusters your errors by root cause, builds a spaced repetition queue, and adapts its difficulty. Every session makes the next one better.

1

Adaptive Difficulty

Computes your real level from grammar mastery and error rate. Pushes harder content as you improve.

2

Spaced Repetition

Prioritizes patterns you struggle with and steers conversation toward them at optimal intervals.

3

L1 Interference

Identifies errors caused by native language habits and explains why you make the mistake.

4

Second-Pass Review

After each session, an outside agent reviews the full conversation to catch missed errors.

5

Tutor Self-Evaluation

Grades its own responses for correction quality and missed teaching moments.

6

Error Clustering

Groups related errors by root cause with specific fix recommendations instead of isolated mistakes.

7

Vocab Tracking

When you ask what a word means, it's saved. Builds a personal dictionary of words you're learning.

8

Topic Difficulty

Each conversation is scored for difficulty so future topic suggestions match your current level.

9

Interest Profiling

Detects your interests from conversations and generates personalized topics you actually want to discuss.

Get Started

Running in under a minute.

setup
# Clone and install $ git clone https://github.com/jeeminhan/open-language $ cd open-language $ pip install -r requirements.txt # Add your API key $ echo "LLM_API_KEY=your-key-here" > .env # Start learning $ python main.py # Optional: launch the dashboard $ cd dashboard && npm run dev
Default LLM: Gemini 2.5 Flash
Also works with: OpenAI, Groq, Ollama
Any language pair: Korean → English, etc.
